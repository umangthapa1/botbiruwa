About Us

Our Story

Founded with a passion for bringing nature into every home, Bot Biruwa has been connecting people with the perfect plants since 2020. We believe that everyone deserves to experience the joy and benefits of living with plants.

Our Mission

To make plant parenthood accessible, enjoyable, and rewarding for everyone. We carefully select each plant in our collection, ensuring they're healthy, beautiful, and perfect for your space.

Our Promise

Quality plants, expert advice, and ongoing support to help your green friends thrive. We're here to guide you every step of your plant journey.
